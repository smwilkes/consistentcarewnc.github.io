Welcome

Consistent Care WNC is dedicated to providing compassionate, inclusive, and patient-centered healthcare services throughout Western North Carolina. Our care model focuses on meeting individuals and families where they are while supporting long-term health, function, and quality of life.

Services

  • Primary and preventive healthcare services
  • Chronic condition management
  • Care coordination and patient advocacy
  • Functional and wellness-focused care planning
  • Community-based and patient education support

Services are tailored to each individual’s needs with an emphasis on continuity, accessibility, and trust.

Our Approach

Healthcare should be accessible, respectful, and rooted in the community. Our approach focuses on collaboration, education, and evidence-informed decision-making, prioritizing patient goals and overall well-being.
